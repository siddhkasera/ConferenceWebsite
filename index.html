Overview

The conference seeks to bring together an interdisciplinary group of scientists, engineers, and social scientists interested in diffusion and related spreading phenomena. Oral presentations will address a broad, interdisciplinary audience.

Topics of interest include
Basic principles of diffusion theory and simulation Experimental measurements and applications in biology, chemistry, physics, and engineering Spreading phenomena in archaeology, ecology, epidemics, linguistics, sociology, etc.
